The other clowns whisper in hushed tones about a mysterious figure that roams the grounds at night, its presence sending shivers down their spines. I've tried to reassure them that it's just their imagination running wild, but deep down, I can't shake the feeling that there's something lurking in the shadows, waiting to reveal itself when we least expect it.
