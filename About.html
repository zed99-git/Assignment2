Step right up and discover the enchanting history of WonderWorld Funhouse! Established in 1995 by a team of passionate dreamers, our funhouse has been a beacon of joy and laughter for families and thrill-seekers alike. From our humble beginnings as a small carnival attraction to our current status as a premier destination for whimsical adventures, WonderWorld has delighted generations of visitors with its imaginative attractions and magical ambiance. At WonderWorld Funhouse, our mission is simple: to ignite the spark of wonder and imagination in the hearts of all who enter our doors. With a dedication to creativity, innovation, and unparalleled guest experiences, we strive to create memories that last a lifetime. Our values of inclusivity, kindness, and fun guide everything we do, ensuring that each visitor leaves with a smile on their face and a skip in their step.
